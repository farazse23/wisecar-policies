Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 20, 2025

At WiseCar, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use our EV charging management application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information
  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Authentication tokens (Google Sign-In)
  • Account creation date
Device & Usage Data
  • Charging device information (model, serial number)
  • Charging session data (duration, energy consumed, costs)
  • Device settings and configurations
  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Error logs and crash reports
Technical Information
  • IP address and network information
  • Device type, OS version, and app version
  • Location data (when explicitly granted)
  • NFC card identifiers
  • WiFi network names (for device setup)
Communication Data
  • AI chat conversations and command history
  • Community posts and comments
  • Customer support interactions
  • Push notification preferences
  • Survey responses and feedback

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Registration: Email, name, and authentication through Google Sign-In
  • Device Setup: Device names, locations, and configuration preferences
  • AI Interactions: Questions, commands, and feedback to our AI assistant
  • Community Participation: Posts, comments, and discussions in WiseCar Plus community
  • Support Requests: Issues, questions, and feedback submitted to customer support

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Charging Session Analytics

We automatically collect detailed information about your charging sessions to provide energy recommendations and cost optimization:

  • Start/end times and duration
  • Energy consumed (kWh) and power levels
  • Current and voltage measurements
  • Device temperature and status
  • Cost calculations based on your electricity rates

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Service Functions

  • Device Control: Enable remote monitoring and control of your WiseCar charging devices
  • User Authentication: Secure login and account management through Firebase Auth
  • Multi-User Access: Manage sharing permissions for family members and friends
  • Session Tracking: Record and analyze your charging history and patterns

2.2 AI-Powered Features

  • Smart Recommendations: Provide personalized energy-saving suggestions using GPT-4o-mini
  • Device Diagnostics: Analyze performance data to identify potential issues
  • Cost Optimization: Calculate optimal charging schedules based on electricity rates
  • Natural Language Processing: Understand and respond to your voice and text commands

2.3 Premium Services (WiseCar Plus)

  • Advanced Analytics: Detailed energy reports and charging insights
  • Priority Support: Faster response times for technical assistance
  • Extended Warranty: Track and manage extended warranty coverage
  • Community Access: Enable participation in exclusive member discussions

4. Data Security & Protection

Encryption & Transit

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3. WebSocket connections to charging devices use secure protocols.

Cloud Security

Data is stored on Google Cloud Platform with enterprise-grade security, including encryption at rest and multi-factor authentication for admin access.

Access Controls

Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access your data, and all access is logged and monitored.

Regular Audits

We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments to maintain the highest level of protection.

Security Measures

256-bit SSL Encryption Firebase Security Rules OAuth 2.0 Authentication GDPR Compliant SOC 2 Type II

5. Data Retention

5.1 Active Accounts

  • Account Information: Retained while your account is active
  • Charging Session Data: Kept for 3 years for analytics and warranty purposes
  • AI Chat History: Stored for 1 year to improve AI responses
  • Device Configurations: Maintained while devices are registered to your account

5.2 Account Deletion

  • When you delete your account, most personal data is removed within 30 days
  • Some data may be retained longer for legal compliance or fraud prevention
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely
  • Backup systems may retain data for up to 90 days after deletion

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access & Control

  • Account Settings: View and edit your profile information directly in the app
  • Data Export: Request a copy of your personal data in machine-readable format
  • Data Deletion: Delete your account and associated data through app settings
  • Correction: Update incorrect information through your profile or by contacting support

6.2 Communication Preferences

  • Push Notifications: Control notification types through device settings
  • Email Communications: Opt out of promotional emails (account-related emails may still be sent)
  • AI Data Usage: Opt out of having your conversations used to improve AI models

6.3 Regional Rights

European Union (GDPR)

  • Right to access, rectify, erase, or port your data
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint with supervisory authorities

California (CCPA/CPRA)

  • Right to know what personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Right to delete personal information (subject to exceptions)
  • Right to opt out of sale (we don't sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

7. Children's Privacy

WiseCar is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete such information.

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data transfers
